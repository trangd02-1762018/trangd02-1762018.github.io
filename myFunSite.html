Second day - Coit Tower

12.19.2019

Coit Tower is actually a slender white concrete column rising from the top of Telegraph Hill, which symbolizes for San Francisco's skyline. The tower is named after Lillie Hitchcock Coit, who is a weathy eccentric and patron of the city's firefighters. He left his bequest to this tower. We paid 9 dollars each to get up there and see the majestic view of San Francisco.
